### Strategies for Winning at Splendor


Splendor is a board game where players embark on the journey of building a jewelry empire and winning the favor of the noble class. The game involves utilizing development cards, gem tokens and noble tiles. The ultimate goal is to accumulate prestige points as possible by obtaining development cards and impressing nobles to join your cause.

### Basic Tips for Playing Splendor


Before we dive into the strategies, let’s go over some basic tips to help you get started:
  To become an expert gem collector you must gather gems in order to purchase cards. These cards are essential for acquiring cards, earning points and ultimately acquiring Nobles for additional bonus points. During a player's turn they have the option to select two gems of the same color if there are at least four of that color remaining. Alternatively they can choose one gem from three colors. Additionally players receive a gem that can be used as any color when reserving a card they are unable or choose not to buy immediately.
    
  Understanding the objective in Splendor; When you first start playing Splendor it's tempting to prioritize collecting gems and cards to create a gem card engine. The game encourages this approach especially when you're still learning. However it's essential not to lose sight of Splendors goal. Despite its simplicity the winner is not determined by who builds the impressive engine; instead it is the player who reaches a minimum of 15 points first.



### Strategies for Winning at Splendor:
  ## Strategy 1: Reserve Cards You Want Early On
  One effective tactic in card games is to reserve your desired cards ensuring you acquire them before your opponents have a chance. This approach becomes particularly advantageous when you aim to construct a combination   centered around colors or card types. By reserving a card you remove it from the playing area. Place it in front of yourself preventing your rivals from purchasing it. Additionally reserving a card allows you to hold      onto it for use if you currently lack gems to make the purchase.

  ## Strategy 2: Take 3 Chips in Early Turns
  During the stages of the game it's often advisable to opt for acquiring three chips or directly purchasing a card. This approach enables you to swiftly accumulate a collection of gems and grants you flexibility in stages. These chips can be utilized either to acquire cards or to save up for the purchase of a noble tile.
  
  ## Strategy 3: Choose Two Colors That You Will Own and Buy Them Up
  One effective strategy for acquiring the cards you desire and impeding your opponents is to select two colors and purchase them exclusively. By concentrating on obtaining all the cards in those chosen colors, such as blue and green you can accumulate a number of points while simultaneously limiting your opponents access to those colors.

  ## Strategy 4: Keep Track of Your Opponents’ Cards
  It's crucial to keep tabs on your opponents cards while playing Splendor. By knowing which cards they are aiming for you can strategically hinder their progress by purchasing those cards before them or reserving them for yourself. Additionally this knowledge allows you to anticipate which nobles they are pursuing and attempt to attract them before your opponents have the chance to do so.
  
### Conclusion

Splendor is a game that demands meticulous planning and strategic thought. By adhering to these strategies and suggestions you'll make progress, towards establishing a jewelry empire and garnering the admiration of the noble class.
