As the Executive Director of Management for One Suryan, a student organization that empowers the South Asian community worldwide, I was responsible for overseeing the overall operations and strategy of the organization. I led a team of talented and passionate students who worked on various aspects of the organization, such as graphic design, research, writing, and podcasting. I also created and maintained the website for One Suryan using HTML, CSS, and PHP. I ensured that the website is dynamic, responsive, and secure, and that it showcases the mission, vision, resources, and projects of the organization.

Working on the website and being with One Suryan has taught me alot. I have learned a lot about web development, social justice, and South Asian culture. I have also improved my skills in leadership, communication, and collaboration. I am grateful for the opportunity to contribute to the organization and to the community. I hope that the website I created will inspire and inform others about the issues and opportunities that South Asians face around the world.
